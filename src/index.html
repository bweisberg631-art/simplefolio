About Me

Call me Ben

I'm a full-stack developer and web designer with over 5 years of experience building online products. I care deeply about what I do, whether I'm writing code, designing brand material, or working one on one with clients.

My background spans software engineering, web development, project management, video production, visual design and more. I bring an end-to-end perspective on digital projects. I've worked with startups, agencies, and independent companies.

I bring a positive, solutions-first mindset to everything I do, not just thinking about short-term achievement but what sets the whole team up for success down the road. As well as adhering to simplicity with frequent collaberation and communication to assure projects run smoothly.
